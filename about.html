About us

About Us

Embed Technologies is an Export Oriented company registered in Visakhapatnam Special Economic Zone (VSEZ) at Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, India. Being a part of the Embed family ensures a broad selection of benefits. Here are a few more:

  • From an employee's first day at Embed Technologies Software, there is an opportunity to work with prominent customers. While teaming with such companies you'll come face to face with complex technological problems and other professional challenges. As you go from one project to another, you'll emerge a highly respected professional. On-site opportunities will give you international exposure and there is the potential to be involved with product architecting and project management, as well. Embed Technologies’ policy of constant innovation also helps you accumulate greater knowledge and skills. All this exposure and opportunities keep your career firmly on the grow path.

  • Embed Technologies is known to be an organization that believes in ethics and values while staying focused on professional goals. We believe that work is nothing without integrity and customers should derive maximum value while working with us.

  • You will find the work atmosphere friendly, open and motivating. To help bring the best out of you, we allow flexible working hours. You can also look forward to being on a first-name basis with the industry's best talents. We believe that learning is a long, upward-spiraling curve that leads to new skill development. It is no wonder that the best learning initiatives are found right here.

  • Our leadership programs prepare you to take on more challenging roles. Or let you slip in and out of roles with relative ease. If you think of yourself as a creative spirit, then you'll simply love the opportunities at our innovation center.

Vision

Our vision is to lay the path between the technology and the end user, providing high quality IT solutions to our clients by combining the technology skills, process focus, domain expertise and a commitment to long-term client relationships. We always try to improve the quality of our products by exploring innovative ideas.
